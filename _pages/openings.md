I am looking for motivated students and postdoctoral researchers interested in theoretical condensed matter physics, quantum materials, and computational many-body physics.

Research in the group will broadly focus on strongly correlated and topological quantum matter.

Projects may combine analytical and numerical approaches, including neural-network quantum states and other modern computational methods.

Prospective students and postdoctoral candidates are encouraged to contact me by email with a CV and a brief description of their research interests.
